My love for real estate investing started when I was a teen and read Rich Dad, Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki. I then went on to earn a degree in Personal Financial Planning from Haworth College of Business at Western Michigan University, which, at that time, had the best finance program in the country. Since then, I have constantly sought out education on the mindset and financial practices of the wealthy.
